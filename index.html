Rick Sanchez

Richard "Rick" Sanchez is the co-eponymous main character and leading protagonist of the show. He is a genius scientist whose alcoholism and reckless, sociopathic behavior are a source of concern for his daughter's family over the safety of their son, Morty.

Rick and Morty is an American animated television series created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon. The show premiered on Cartoon Network's [adult swim] block on December 2, 2013.
